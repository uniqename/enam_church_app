Privacy Policy

Faith Klinik Ministries — Columbus, Ohio
Effective Date: January 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: April 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Faith Klinik app:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, and role within the church community.
  • Member directory data: department, join date, and ministry roles (provided voluntarily).
  • Usage data: app interactions, sermon views, and event attendance (stored locally or in our secure database).
  • Photos: profile photos and group photos uploaded by you (stored in Supabase cloud storage).
  • Children's accounts: age (year only) and a parent-assigned PIN for children's ministry access. No personal data beyond age is collected for children.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your profile in the church member directory.
  • To send church announcements and event notifications.
  • To personalize content (adult or children's ministry view) based on account type.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), a SOC 2 compliant cloud database provider. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted to authorized church administrators.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The children's section of this app is intended for use by church members' children under parental supervision. Children's accounts are created by parents or guardians. We collect only the child's age (in years) to provide age-appropriate content. We do not collect names, emails, or any other personally identifiable information for children. Parents may contact us at info@faithklinikministries.com to request deletion of any children's account data.

5. Giving and Financial Data

The app links to Givelify (givelify.com), a third-party giving platform, for tithes and offerings. All financial transactions are processed by Givelify and are subject to their privacy policy. We do not store payment card information in our app or database.

6. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by:

  • Emailing: info@faithklinikministries.com
  • Visiting our data deletion page: faithklinik.app/delete

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
